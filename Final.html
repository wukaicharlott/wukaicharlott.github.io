Citizens born in China

From the data in 2011, the intensity for Chinese population is mostly around the river bank or somewhere away from the city central. The reason behind that is because of the expensive housing price and the high population amount for Chinese people in Sydney. Comparing with the data in 2016, in 2016 the top 10 areas for birth in country is somewhere around the school area. Sydney has UNSW and USYD, the students from china are more preferring live close to campus or the lower rent place.

Citizens born in Philippines

The distribution of Philippine population from 2011 to 2016 is almost maintain the same. Both are highly located around outer-south of Sydney. The Philippian people always act as labour, which can only get lower salary than other jobs. Because of the lower payment, the space for them to afford is limited around area like St Clair. These areas don’t ask for high rent. Moreover, the high labour area is around the outer area in Sydney, so that place is close to their working area.
